SOME QUESTIONS YOU MIGHT NEED TO ASK
— Doubts, Doubts, Doubts
What do I have to do to work with you?

Send us an email to hola@freehouse.es (or give us a call) and tell us about your idea. We will answer and arrange a meeting through Skype. Easy peasy!

Can I work with you just for a part of the project?

Of course! If you already have a team, outsourced part of the project with another company or you just need one of the services we provide, you can choose whatever suits you best.

What happens if I have a big idea but a not so big investment?

Tell us about your idea! If it is such a great idea, we won’t let it go. It is why and how the Freehouse exists.

What’s the Freehouse?

It's our incubation space. Our co-living and co-working place. A home where you can develop your project, interact with our team and every entrepreneur who comes.

How many projects do you build up every year?

Regarding that, we are as exclusive as a boutique. We only accept 2 projects per year. The best ones.
